Crowdfunding for Sports Teams

Project Description

Design a web application for people to crowd-fund donations for charities and foundations, and allow people to donate to specific causes.

Logo Design

I began this project by selecting a market that would require me to work within constraints. I decided sports would be a great market to design for because it requires a certain energy to be conveyed. When developing the logo, I was searching for a way to unite sports with fundraising. The use of the pennant “flying” as a flag helps to bring these two concepts together.


Final Logo
Additional Concepts

I choose a bold slab-serif typeface (Crete Round) reminiscent of School athletic typefaces. Aligning the pennant to the vertical slant of the “t” grounds the shape and reinforces the concept of unity. The corners of the pennant shape were rounded to create cohesion with the typeface
